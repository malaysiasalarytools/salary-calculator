How to Use This EIS Calculator

Enter your monthly salary and choose the EIS rate. The calculator will estimate employee EIS deduction, employer EIS contribution and total EIS contribution.

EIS Contribution = Monthly Wage × EIS Rate Example: RM4,000 × 0.2% = RM8 employee EIS deduction

Employee EIS vs Employer EIS

Employee EIS is deducted from salary. Employer EIS is paid by the employer on top of salary. Both are part of the employment insurance contribution system.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is EIS calculated in Malaysia?

EIS is commonly estimated using a small percentage of monthly wages for both employee and employer contributions, subject to official contribution rules.

Is employer EIS deducted from my salary?

No. Employee EIS is deducted from salary. Employer EIS is paid separately by the employer.

Why is my actual EIS different from this calculator?

Actual payroll may use official EIS contribution tables and rounding rules, so it can differ slightly from a simple percentage estimate.

Does EIS affect take-home pay?

Yes. Employee EIS is a salary deduction, so it slightly reduces monthly take-home pay.
