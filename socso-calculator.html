What is SOCSO in Malaysia?

SOCSO, also known as PERKESO, is Malaysia's social security organisation. It provides protection for employees against workplace injuries, occupational diseases, disability and other employment-related risks.

SOCSO contributions are shared between employers and employees. The contribution amount depends on monthly wages and follows the official SOCSO contribution schedule.

SOCSO benefits may include medical support, disability benefits, dependant benefits and rehabilitation assistance for eligible workers.

Malaysia SOCSO Contribution Rates 2026

SOCSO contributions are shared between employers and employees. The contribution amount depends on the employee's monthly wages and follows the contribution schedule issued by PERKESO.
