Frequently Asked Questions

Are public holidays paid in Malaysia?

Many employees are entitled to paid public holidays, subject to employment terms, company policy and applicable rules.

Is this calculator exact?

No. This calculator provides a simple estimate only. Actual entitlement may depend on state holidays, company policy and employment terms.

Can I include annual leave?

Yes. You can enter annual leave days, public holidays and other paid leave days.

Why do public holiday days differ by company?

Public holidays may depend on federal holidays, state holidays, company policy and the employer's selected holiday list.

Does this calculate exact working days?

It gives a simple estimate using 365 days minus weekends and paid days off. Actual working days may differ based on schedule, state holidays and company calendar.
