What Is A Public Holiday Calculator?

A public holiday calculator helps estimate total paid days off by combining annual leave, public holidays and other paid leave days.

In Malaysia, employees may receive paid annual leave and paid public holidays depending on employment terms, company policy and applicable regulations.

How Is Total Paid Leave Calculated?

This calculator adds annual leave days, public holiday days and other paid leave days together.

The formula used is:

Total Paid Days Off = Annual Leave + Public Holidays + Other Paid Leave

Example Public Holiday Calculation

If an employee has 12 annual leave days, 18 public holidays and 2 other paid leave days, the total paid days off is 32 days.
