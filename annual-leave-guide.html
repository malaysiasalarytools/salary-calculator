What Is Annual Leave?

Annual leave is paid time off that an employee can take during the year while still receiving salary.

Annual leave helps employees rest, handle personal matters and maintain work-life balance without losing income.

Annual Leave Entitlement In Malaysia

Annual leave entitlement may depend on length of service, employment contract, company policy and applicable employment law.

A commonly used minimum entitlement structure is:

  • Less than 2 years of service: 8 days
  • 2 to 5 years of service: 12 days
  • More than 5 years of service: 16 days

Some companies may offer more annual leave than the minimum requirement.

How Is Annual Leave Calculated?

For employees who work for a full year, annual leave is usually based on their yearly entitlement.

For employees who join or leave during the year, annual leave may be pro-rated based on the number of months worked.

Pro-Rated Annual Leave Formula

A simple pro-rated leave formula is:

Pro-Rated Leave = Annual Leave Entitlement × Months Worked ÷ 12

This provides an estimate of leave entitlement for employees who have not completed a full year of service.

Example Annual Leave Calculation

Assume an employee is entitled to 12 days of annual leave per year and worked 6 months during the year.

The estimated pro-rated annual leave is:

12 × 6 ÷ 12 = 6 days

This means the employee may be entitled to approximately 6 days of annual leave for that period.

Annual Leave And Public Holidays

Annual leave is different from public holidays.

Public holidays are generally fixed holiday dates, while annual leave is usually taken based on employee application and employer approval.

Employees may use both annual leave and public holidays to plan longer breaks during the year.

Annual Leave And Resignation

When an employee resigns, unused annual leave may need to be handled according to employment contract, company policy and applicable rules.

Some employees may use annual leave during notice period, while others may receive payment for unused leave depending on the situation.

Can Annual Leave Be Carried Forward?

Carry forward rules depend on company policy and employment terms.

Some employers allow unused leave to be carried forward to the next year, while others may require employees to use leave before a deadline.

Tips For Employees

  • Check your employment contract
  • Track leave taken during the year
  • Apply for leave early where possible
  • Understand carry forward policy
  • Check unused leave before resigning

Frequently Asked Questions

How many annual leave days do employees get in Malaysia?

A common minimum entitlement structure is 8, 12 or 16 days depending on length of service.

Can annual leave be pro-rated?

Yes. Annual leave may be pro-rated when an employee joins or leaves during the year.

Is annual leave paid?

Annual leave is generally paid leave, subject to employment terms and applicable rules.

Is this guide legal advice?
