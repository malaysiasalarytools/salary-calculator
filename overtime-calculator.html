What Is Overtime Pay In Malaysia?

Overtime pay is additional pay given to employees who work beyond normal working hours. In Malaysia, overtime calculation may depend on salary, working hours, employment category and applicable labour law.

How Is Overtime Pay Calculated?

A common simple estimate is to calculate hourly rate from monthly salary, then multiply it by the overtime rate and overtime hours.

This calculator uses the formula: monthly salary divided by 26 working days, divided by 8 hours, then multiplied by 1.5 times.

Example Overtime Calculation

If an employee earns RM3,000 per month, the estimated hourly rate is RM3,000 ÷ 26 ÷ 8.

If the employee works 10 overtime hours, the estimated OT pay is hourly rate × 1.5 × 10 hours.
