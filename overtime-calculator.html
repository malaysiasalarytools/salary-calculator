How Overtime Pay Is Calculated in Malaysia

Overtime pay is additional pay given to employees who work beyond normal working hours. A simple estimate is to calculate the hourly rate from monthly salary, then multiply it by the overtime rate and overtime hours.

OT Pay = Monthly Salary ÷ Working Days ÷ Hours Per Day × OT Rate × OT Hours Example: RM3,000 ÷ 26 ÷ 8 × 1.5 × 10 hours = estimated OT pay

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this overtime calculator exact?

No. This calculator provides a simplified estimate only. Actual overtime pay may vary depending on employment contract, salary coverage, company policy and applicable regulations.

What overtime rate is used?

The default estimate uses 1.5x. You can also select 2.0x, 3.0x or 1.0x depending on the type of overtime estimate you want.

Can overtime affect take-home pay?

Yes. Overtime increases gross income and may affect payroll deductions, PCB tax and final annual income tax.

Can I calculate monthly overtime?

Yes. Enter your monthly salary and overtime hours to estimate total OT pay for the month.

Can I change working days or hours per day?

Yes. This calculator lets you adjust the working days per month and normal hours per day to match your estimate.
