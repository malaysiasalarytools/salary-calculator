About Malaysia Salary Tools

Malaysia Salary Tools is an independent online platform created to help Malaysians better understand salary, payroll, tax, employment and financial planning calculations.

Why We Built Malaysia Salary Tools

Many employees in Malaysia receive their monthly salary without fully understanding how their take-home pay is calculated. EPF, SOCSO, EIS, PCB tax, overtime pay, leave entitlement and other payroll items can be confusing, especially when the information is spread across different websites, tables and official documents.

Malaysia Salary Tools was created to bring these topics together in one simple and easy-to-use platform. Our goal is to help users make quick estimates, understand common payroll terms and learn about salary-related matters in a clear and practical way.

We focus on building free calculators and beginner-friendly guides for Malaysian employees, job seekers, HR users, payroll teams, business owners and anyone who wants to understand salary and employment matters more clearly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make salary, payroll, tax and employment information easier to understand for everyone in Malaysia.

We believe that users should be able to estimate their salary deductions, compare job offers, check payroll components and understand employment-related topics without needing advanced tax or payroll knowledge.

Information Sources

Malaysia Salary Tools is not a government website. However, where applicable, our calculators and guides are prepared with reference to publicly available information from relevant Malaysian authorities and official sources.

Common official references include:

  • KWSP / EPF for employee and employer contribution matters
  • PERKESO / SOCSO for social security contribution information
  • LHDN for income tax, PCB, tax filing, tax relief and rebate matters
  • Ministry of Human Resources for employment and labour-related information
