About Malaysia Salary Tools

Malaysia Salary Tools is a free online platform that provides salary, tax and payroll calculators for employees, employers, freelancers and job seekers in Malaysia.

Our goal is to make salary-related calculations simple, fast and easy to understand.

What We Provide

We provide tools such as Salary Calculator, EPF Calculator, SOCSO Calculator, EIS Calculator, PCB Calculator, Income Tax Calculator, Overtime Calculator, Bonus Calculator, Payslip Generator and other employment-related calculators.

Important Disclaimer

The calculators and guides on this website are for general informational purposes only. They should not be considered professional tax, legal, payroll or financial advice.

Users should refer to official government sources, employers, payroll providers or qualified professionals for accurate and up-to-date advice.

Our Mission

We aim to help Malaysians better understand salary, deductions, tax, leave entitlement and other employment-related matters.
