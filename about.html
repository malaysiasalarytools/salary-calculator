# About Malaysia Salary Tools Malaysia Salary Tools is a free online platform that provides salary, tax and payroll calculators for employees, employers and job seekers in Malaysia. Our goal is to make salary calculations simple and accessible for everyone. We provide tools such as Salary Calculator, EPF Calculator, SOCSO Calculator, EIS Calculator, PCB Calculator and other employment-related calculators. The information provided on this website is for general informational purposes only and should not be considered professional tax, legal or financial advice. We continuously improve our calculators and guides to help Malaysians better understand their salary, tax and employment benefits. Thank you for visiting Malaysia Salary Tools.
