What Is Salary Deduction?

Salary deduction means an amount is deducted from an employee's gross salary before the final net salary is paid. Deductions can include statutory contributions, tax, unpaid leave, salary advances or other approved deductions.

This guide is written as a practical overview for employees, job seekers, HR staff and small business owners. Actual treatment may depend on employment contract terms, company policy and the latest legal requirements.

What is salary deduction in Malaysia?
