What Is A Public Holiday?

A public holiday is a recognised holiday where many employees may be entitled to a day off or holiday pay depending on their employment terms and applicable rules.

National vs State Public Holidays

Malaysia has national public holidays and state-specific public holidays.

Some holidays apply across Malaysia, while others apply only in certain states.

Public Holiday And Annual Leave

Public holidays are different from annual leave.

Annual leave is usually applied by employees and approved by employers, while public holidays are set according to holiday calendars and applicable rules.

Replacement Public Holiday

If a public holiday falls on a rest day, some employers may provide a replacement holiday depending on employment terms and applicable rules.

Employees should check company policy or HR guidance for how replacement holidays are handled.

Working On Public Holiday

Employees who work on a public holiday may be entitled to additional pay or replacement benefits depending on employment terms and applicable rules.

The exact treatment may vary by job type, working schedule and employer policy.

How To Plan Leave Around Public Holidays

Employees often combine annual leave with public holidays to create longer breaks.

For example, taking one or two annual leave days before or after a public holiday may create a long weekend.

Example Leave Planning

If a public holiday falls on Friday, an employee may take annual leave on Thursday to create a four-day break including the weekend.

Tips For Employees

  • Check the public holiday calendar early
  • Apply annual leave in advance
  • Understand replacement holiday rules
  • Confirm holiday treatment with HR
  • Track annual leave balance

Tips For Employers

  • Publish holiday calendar early
  • Clarify replacement holiday policy
  • Manage staffing for holiday periods
  • Ensure payroll treatment is accurate

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all public holidays the same in every Malaysian state?

No. Some holidays are national while others are state-specific.

Can I combine annual leave with public holidays?

Yes, subject to employer approval and company policy.

Do public holidays reduce annual leave?

Public holidays and annual leave are generally separate, but exact treatment depends on employment terms.

Is this guide official legal advice?

No. It is for general information only.
