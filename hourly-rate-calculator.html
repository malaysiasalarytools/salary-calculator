What Is Hourly Rate?

Hourly rate is the amount an employee earns for each hour of work. It is useful for calculating overtime pay, part-time wages and daily payroll estimates.

In Malaysia, employees and employers may use hourly rate calculations to estimate pay for overtime, unpaid leave, part-time work or salary comparison.

How To Convert Monthly Salary To Hourly Rate

To estimate hourly rate, divide monthly salary by working days per month, then divide by working hours per day.

Formula:

Hourly Rate = Monthly Salary ÷ Working Days ÷ Working Hours Per Day

Example Hourly Rate Calculation

If your monthly salary is RM5,000, you work 26 days per month and 8 hours per day, your estimated hourly rate is:

RM5,000 ÷ 26 ÷ 8 = RM24.04 per hour.
