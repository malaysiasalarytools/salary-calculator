How Hourly Rate Is Calculated

Hourly rate is the amount an employee earns for each hour of work. It is useful for calculating overtime pay, part-time wages, unpaid leave, daily pay and salary comparison.

Hourly Rate = Monthly Salary ÷ Working Days ÷ Working Hours Per Day Example: RM5,000 ÷ 26 ÷ 8 = RM24.04 per hour

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate hourly rate from monthly salary?

Divide your monthly salary by working days per month, then divide by working hours per day.

Is this hourly rate calculator exact?

No. This calculator provides a simplified estimate only. Actual payroll calculation may depend on company policy, employment contract and applicable rules.

Can I use this for overtime calculation?

Yes. Hourly rate is commonly used to estimate overtime pay. You can use the overtime calculator after getting your hourly rate.

What working days should I use?

Many simple Malaysian payroll estimates use 26 working days, but this may vary depending on company policy, work schedule and contract terms.

Can I calculate daily rate too?

Yes. This calculator shows both estimated daily rate and hourly rate.
