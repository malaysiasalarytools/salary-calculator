What Is MC Leave In Malaysia?

MC leave, also known as medical leave or sick leave, is paid leave given to employees who are unable to work due to illness and have a valid medical certificate.

Medical leave entitlement in Malaysia may depend on length of service, employment terms and applicable employment law.

How Is MC Leave Calculated?

This calculator subtracts MC days used from annual medical leave entitlement.

Formula:

Remaining MC Leave = MC Entitlement - MC Days Used

Malaysia Medical Leave Entitlement

  • Less than 2 years of service: 14 days
  • 2 to 5 years of service: 18 days
  • More than 5 years of service: 22 days

Example MC Leave Calculation

If an employee is entitled to 14 days of MC and has used 5 days, the remaining MC balance is 9 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MC leave paid in Malaysia?

Medical leave is generally paid when supported by a valid medical certificate, subject to employment terms and applicable rules.

How many MC days are employees entitled to?

Common entitlement levels are 14, 18 or 22 days depending on length of service.

Is this calculator exact?

This calculator provides an estimate only. Actual entitlement may depend on company policy and employment contract.
