How MC Leave Is Calculated in Malaysia

MC leave, also known as medical leave or sick leave, is paid leave given to employees who are unable to work due to illness and have a valid medical certificate.

Remaining MC Leave = MC Entitlement − MC Days Used Example: 14 days entitlement − 5 days used = 9 days remaining

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MC leave paid in Malaysia?

Medical leave is generally paid when supported by a valid medical certificate, subject to employment terms, company policy and applicable rules.

How many MC days are employees entitled to?

Common entitlement levels are 14, 18 or 22 days depending on length of service.

Is this MC leave calculator exact?

No. This calculator provides an estimate only. Actual entitlement may depend on company policy, employment contract and applicable employment law.

What happens if I use more MC than my entitlement?

If MC used is higher than entitlement, the excess may be treated according to company policy, employment contract and applicable rules.

Is hospitalisation leave included here?

No. This calculator focuses on basic non-hospitalisation MC leave balance. Hospitalisation leave may have separate rules and entitlement.
