What Is A Notice Period?

A notice period is the amount of time an employee must continue working after submitting a resignation before leaving the company.

The required notice period is usually stated in the employment contract.

How Is Last Working Day Calculated?

The calculator adds the notice period to the resignation date to estimate the final working day.

Example

If you resign on 1 January and your notice period is 30 days, your estimated last working day would be 31 January.
