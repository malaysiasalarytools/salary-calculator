How Bonus Tax Is Estimated in Malaysia

Bonus payments are generally treated as taxable employment income in Malaysia. This means a bonus can increase your annual taxable income and may result in higher PCB deduction or final income tax.

Net Bonus = Gross Bonus − Estimated Bonus Tax − Other Bonus Deductions This calculator uses a simplified bonus tax rate for planning only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is bonus taxable in Malaysia?

Yes. Bonus is generally considered taxable employment income in Malaysia and may increase your annual taxable income.

Does bonus affect PCB?

Yes. A bonus may increase annual income and may increase PCB deduction, especially in the month where bonus is paid.

Is this bonus calculator exact?

No. This calculator provides a simplified estimate only. Actual PCB may vary depending on official calculation, payroll settings, tax reliefs and personal circumstances.

Does EPF apply to bonus?

Bonus treatment may depend on payroll policy and applicable statutory rules. Employers should refer to current payroll guidelines and official requirements.

Why is my net bonus lower than expected?

Your net bonus may be lower because of PCB, EPF treatment, other payroll deductions or final annual tax impact.
