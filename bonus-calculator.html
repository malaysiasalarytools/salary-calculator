What Is Bonus Tax In Malaysia?

Bonus payments are generally treated as taxable employment income in Malaysia. This means a bonus may increase your annual taxable income and may result in additional PCB deductions.

The exact tax on bonus depends on total annual income, available tax reliefs and the applicable tax calculation method.

How Is Bonus Tax Calculated?

Bonus tax is usually considered together with your employment income for the year. If your bonus increases your annual taxable income, your estimated PCB deduction may also increase.

This calculator provides a simplified estimate of bonus tax based on your monthly salary and bonus amount.

Example Bonus Calculation

For example, if you earn RM5,000 per month and receive a RM10,000 bonus, your estimated annual income becomes RM70,000.

Depending on the estimated tax rate, part of your bonus may be deducted as PCB tax. The remaining amount is your estimated net bonus.
