What Is Annual Leave In Malaysia?

Annual leave is paid leave that employees may take during the year while still receiving salary. In Malaysia, minimum annual leave entitlement generally depends on the employee's length of service.

How Is Annual Leave Calculated?

A common simple approach is to determine the employee's annual leave entitlement based on years of service, then pro-rate the entitlement based on the number of months worked during the year.

This calculator uses a simple pro-rated formula:

Annual Leave Entitlement × Months Worked ÷ 12

Malaysia Annual Leave Entitlement

  • Less than 2 years of service: 8 days
  • 2 to 5 years of service: 12 days
  • More than 5 years of service: 16 days

Example Annual Leave Calculation

If an employee is entitled to 12 days of annual leave and worked 6 months in the year, the pro-rated leave entitlement is 12 × 6 ÷ 12 = 6 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this annual leave calculator exact?

This calculator provides an estimate only. Actual entitlement may depend on employment contract, company policy and applicable law.

How many annual leave days do employees get in Malaysia?

Minimum annual leave entitlement commonly depends on length of service: 8, 12 or 16 days.

Can annual leave be pro-rated?

Yes. Annual leave is often pro-rated when an employee joins or leaves during the year.
