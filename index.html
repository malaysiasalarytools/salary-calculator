How Salary Is Calculated in Malaysia

In Malaysia, your take-home salary is lower than gross salary because of mandatory deductions: EPF, SOCSO, EIS and PCB tax.

Salary Examples

  • RM3,000 → ~RM2,600 take home
  • RM5,000 → ~RM4,200 take home
  • RM10,000 → ~RM7,800 take home
