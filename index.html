📊 What Are The Components Of A Salary Calculator?

Calculating your take-home pay means subtracting statutory deductions from your gross monthly salary. In Malaysia, the main deductions are usually EPF, SOCSO, EIS and PCB.

Net Salary = Gross Salary − Total Deductions Total Deductions = EPF + SOCSO + EIS + PCB
💰

EPF

Employee Provident Fund contribution for retirement savings.

🛡️

SOCSO

Social security protection for employment injury and related risks.

📋

EIS

Employment insurance support for eligible employees who lose their jobs.

🏛️

PCB

Monthly income tax deduction deducted through payroll.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is net salary?

Net salary is the amount received after deductions such as EPF, SOCSO, EIS and PCB.

Is this salary calculator exact?

This calculator provides an estimate. Actual payroll deductions may vary depending on official rates, tax reliefs and personal circumstances.

Does EPF reduce take-home pay?

Yes. The employee portion of EPF is deducted from monthly salary.

What is PCB tax?

PCB is Malaysia's monthly income tax deduction system administered through payroll.
