What Deductions Affect Your Salary?

EPF (Employees Provident Fund)

Mandatory retirement savings. Employee contributes ~11% of monthly salary.

SOCSO

Social security protection for workplace injury and disability coverage.

EIS

Employment insurance for job loss financial support.

PCB Tax (Income Tax)

Monthly tax deduction based on progressive income tax system in Malaysia.

Why Your Net Salary Is Lower Than Your Offer Letter

Offer letters show gross salary. Actual bank-in salary is reduced by EPF, SOCSO, EIS and PCB tax deductions.
