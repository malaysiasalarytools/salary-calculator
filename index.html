How Salary Is Calculated in Malaysia

Your gross salary is reduced by mandatory statutory deductions in Malaysia. These include EPF, SOCSO, EIS and PCB (income tax).

What is EPF?

EPF (Employees Provident Fund) is a mandatory retirement savings scheme in Malaysia. Employees contribute about 11% of monthly salary.

What is SOCSO?

SOCSO provides protection for workplace injury, disability and employment safety coverage.

What is EIS?

EIS (Employment Insurance System) provides financial assistance if you lose your job.

What is PCB Tax?

PCB is monthly income tax deduction based on Malaysia’s progressive tax system.
