How to Use This EPF Calculator

Enter your monthly salary, choose the employee EPF rate and employer EPF rate, then click calculate. The calculator will estimate your employee EPF deduction, employer EPF contribution and total EPF contribution.

EPF Contribution = Monthly Salary × EPF Rate Example: RM4,000 × 11% = RM440 employee EPF deduction

Employee EPF vs Employer EPF

Employee EPF is deducted from the employee's salary. Employer EPF is paid by the employer on top of salary. Both amounts contribute to the employee's EPF account.

Note: Actual payroll results may differ slightly because official EPF contribution schedules may use fixed contribution tables instead of simple percentage multiplication.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is EPF calculated in Malaysia?

EPF is generally calculated based on monthly wages and applicable employee and employer contribution rates. This calculator uses percentage estimates for easier planning.

Is employer EPF deducted from my salary?

No. Employee EPF is deducted from your salary. Employer EPF is an additional contribution paid by the employer.

Why is my actual EPF different from this calculator?

Actual EPF may follow the official KWSP contribution schedule and payroll rounding rules, so it may differ slightly from a simple percentage calculation.

Does EPF affect take-home pay?

Yes. Employee EPF reduces your monthly take-home pay, but it goes into your EPF retirement savings account.
