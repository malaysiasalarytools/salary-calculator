What Is a Payslip?

A payslip is a salary document that shows an employee's earnings, deductions and net salary for a specific pay period. In Malaysia, a payslip commonly includes basic salary, allowance, EPF, SOCSO, EIS, PCB and net pay.

Common Earnings

  • Basic salary
  • Fixed allowance
  • Overtime pay
  • Commission or bonus

Common Deductions

  • Employee EPF
  • Employee SOCSO
  • Employee EIS
  • PCB monthly tax deduction
