What Is A Payslip?

A payslip is a salary document that shows an employee's earnings, deductions and net salary for a specific pay period.

In Malaysia, a payslip usually includes basic salary, EPF, SOCSO, EIS, PCB tax deductions and net salary.

Why Use A Payslip Generator?

A payslip generator helps employees, freelancers and small businesses create a simple salary slip quickly.

  • Prepare salary slip preview
  • Calculate net salary
  • Show deductions clearly
  • Useful for simple payroll records

What Should Be Included In A Payslip?

  • Employee name
  • Company name
  • Pay period
  • Basic salary
  • EPF deduction
  • SOCSO deduction
  • EIS deduction
  • PCB tax deduction
  • Net salary
