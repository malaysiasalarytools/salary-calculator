PCB vs Final Income Tax

PCB is a monthly tax deduction from salary. Final income tax is confirmed during annual tax filing after considering total income, reliefs, rebates and PCB already paid.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PCB the same as income tax?

No. PCB is monthly tax deducted through payroll. Final income tax is calculated annually during tax filing.

Why is my actual PCB different from this calculator?

Actual PCB may use LHDN's official formula, TP1 relief updates, spouse or child status, rebates, bonus treatment and payroll settings.

Can tax relief reduce PCB?

Tax relief may reduce PCB if submitted through the proper payroll process, or it may be reflected during annual tax filing.

Does bonus affect PCB?

Yes. Bonus can increase taxable income and may cause higher PCB deduction for the bonus month.
