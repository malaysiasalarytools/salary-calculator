PCB Deduction

PCB is deducted from monthly salary through payroll. It helps spread tax payment across the year instead of paying all tax at once during filing.

Final Income Tax

Final income tax is confirmed during annual e-Filing after total income, reliefs, rebates, zakat and PCB already paid are considered.

PCB vs Final Income Tax

Many employees confuse PCB with final income tax. PCB is only a monthly deduction. Your final tax position is confirmed when you file your annual tax return.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PCB the same as income tax?

No. PCB is monthly tax deducted through payroll. Final income tax is calculated annually during tax filing after considering total income, reliefs, rebates and PCB already paid.

Why is my actual PCB different from this calculator?

Actual PCB may use LHDN's official formula, TP1 / TP3 details, spouse or child status, rebates, zakat, bonus treatment and payroll settings.

Can tax relief reduce PCB?

Tax relief may reduce PCB if it is properly updated through payroll, or it may reduce final tax during annual e-Filing.

Does bonus affect PCB?

Yes. Bonus can increase taxable income and may cause higher PCB deduction, especially in the bonus month.

What is chargeable income?

Chargeable income is the estimated taxable amount after deducting selected reliefs and deductions from annual income.

What happens if I overpay PCB?

If your PCB paid is higher than your final tax payable, you may be eligible for a tax refund after filing your annual tax return.
