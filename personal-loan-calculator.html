Frequently Asked Questions

Is this personal loan calculator exact?

No. This calculator provides an estimate only. Actual repayment may vary depending on bank rate, fees, insurance, loan method and approval terms.

What is the difference between flat rate and reducing balance?

Flat rate calculates interest based on the original loan amount. Reducing balance estimates interest based on outstanding balance over time.

Does longer tenure reduce monthly installment?

Usually yes. Longer tenure may reduce monthly repayment, but total interest paid may be higher.

Should I check DSR before applying for a personal loan?

Yes. DSR helps estimate whether your monthly commitments are reasonable compared with your income.

Does this calculator include processing fee?

You can enter an optional processing fee or upfront cost estimate. Actual fees depend on the lender and loan package.
